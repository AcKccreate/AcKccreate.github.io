4. Biometric-Adjacent Data — BreatheWorld

Our BreatheWorld feature uses device microphone input to detect breath rhythm and pacing patterns for wellness guidance. We do not store raw audio recordings from BreatheWorld sessions. Breath pattern data is processed in real-time on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Aggregated, anonymized pattern statistics may be used to improve the feature.

If you are located in a jurisdiction with biometric data laws (Illinois BIPA, Texas CUBI, Washington My Health MY Data Act, or similar), you may withdraw consent at any time by disabling microphone access in your device settings.
