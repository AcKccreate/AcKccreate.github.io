Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

AnchorWithin LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds free browser-based tools. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Our tools are designed with privacy-first principles — most data never leaves your device.

Data We Collect

Our tools are client-side and store data locally in your browser. We do not operate servers that receive your personal input from tools.

  • Locally stored data: Tool settings, session data, and user preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (e.g., page view counts) that do not include personal identifiers.
  • Contact: If you email us at hello@anchorwithin.com, we store that correspondence to respond to you.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect names, emails, or payment information through our free tools.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or behavioral advertising.

Third-Party Services

Our pages are hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL) per their own privacy policy.

Some tools embed YouTube videos or link to external services. Those services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data server-side, you can clear all locally stored tool data by clearing your browser's localStorage (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data). For any other requests, contact us at hello@anchorwithin.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our tools after changes constitutes acceptance.
