How EchoWalk Works

Your Story, Remembered

EchoWalk uses AI-assisted memory reconstruction to help you revisit, preserve, and share the moments that shaped you.

Step One
Gather Your Echoes
Upload photos, voice memos, dates, and fragments. Even partial memories. EchoWalk works with whatever you have — a street name, a year, a feeling.
Step Two
AI Reconstructs the Walk
Our narrative AI weaves your fragments into a rich, sensory memory walk — filling gaps with period-accurate detail while preserving the emotional truth of your story.
Step Three
Walk Through It
Experience your memory as a guided audio walk, an illustrated digital journal, or a cinematic timeline. Share it with family, or keep it private forever.
Step Four
Preserve It
Export as a beautifully formatted PDF memoir, audio file, or private website. A gift for generations who haven't been born yet.
